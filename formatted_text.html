DCeased:How to do a What-If

What is DCeased

The storyline DCeased is a zombie what-if scenario comic run by DC. The series is strengthened with emphasis on using their underdog characters by immediately killing off fan-favorite characters like Superman and Batman. They changed the zombie virus instead into the ALE which works, best explained by Batman as.


They're not zombies.

They're not consumed by hunger.

They're not feeding.

They're spreading death.

They're stealing life. These are the Anti-Living. And the trigger is an equation

How the story is written unique

My favorite chapter involves all the villain characters swapping their role dynamics. The main events have already occurred, meaning all the heroes are zombies. When the last hero dies, the story then switches the lead onto the villains who then grow into the new heroes of the story. The villains' interests lead them from protecting themselves to raising and protecting a group of orphans who are alone.

Analyzing Appeal

This became a generic unique take on how a zombie story unfolded. This was a trend that I believe DC did the best with, truly making an unpredictable pathing of the story. I would heavily reccomend the comic series DCeased to any DC fans or zombies enjoyers.
